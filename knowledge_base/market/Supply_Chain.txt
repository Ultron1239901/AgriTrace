AgriTrace Supply Chain & Logistics
==================================
Cold chain transport and trace logs preserve crop value.
- Cold Chain: Refrigated vehicles (reefers) are required for perishables (banana, tomato, leafy vegetables).
- First-In, First-Out (FIFO): Ensure older harvest batches are moved first to avoid spoilage.
- Trace Logs: Every logistics hand-off is logged using batch QR codes to ensure transparency.
