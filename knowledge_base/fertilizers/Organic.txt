AgriTrace Organic Fertilizer Guide
==================================
Organic nutrients feed the soil microbiome and improve long-term soil structure.
Key organic sources:
1. Farm Yard Manure (FYM): Well-decomposed cow dung and straw.
2. Vermicompost: High nutrient castings produced by earthworms.
3. Biofertilizers: Nitrogen-fixing Azotobacter/Rhizobium and Phosphate Solubilizing Bacteria (PSB). Apply to seed or soil.
