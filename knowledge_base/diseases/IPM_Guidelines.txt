AgriTrace Integrated Pest Management (IPM) Guidelines
=====================================================
IPM combines cultural, physical, biological, and chemical tools to manage pests sustainably.
1. Cultural: Crop rotation, intercropping, trap cropping (e.g. planting marigold near tomato to divert nematodes).
2. Physical: Handpicking caterpillars, field sanitation, weed clearance.
3. Biological: Releasing Trichogramma egg parasitoids or applying Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt).
4. Chemical: Use only as a last resort under strict thresholds.
