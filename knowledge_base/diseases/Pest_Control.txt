AgriTrace Pest Control Guidelines
=================================
Effective pest management prevents economic crop damage.
Key Sucking Pests: Whiteflies, Thrips, Aphids, Mites. (Vectors for viral diseases).
- Organic Control: Spray 5% Neem Seed Kernel Extract (NSKE), release ladybird beetles, or install yellow/blue sticky traps.
- Chemical Control: Apply Acetamiprid, Dimethoate, or Spinosad following safety guidelines.
