AgriTrace Viral Disease Manual
==============================
Viral plant pathogens are not directly treatable. Control focuses on removing infected plants and managing insect vectors.
Key Diseases:
1. Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl Virus (TYLCV): Stunted growth, curly yellow leaves. Transmitted by Whiteflies. Control vectors using sticky traps, neem oil, or Imidacloprid.
2. Chilli Leaf Curl: Leaves curl upwards. Transmitted by thrips and whiteflies. Spray systemic insecticides.
