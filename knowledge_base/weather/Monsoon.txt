AgriTrace Weather Advisory: Monsoon Management
==============================================
Monsoon rain is critical for Kharif crops like rice, maize, and cotton.
Advisories:
- Drainage: Build proper field channels to drain excess rainwater and prevent crop inundation/root rot.
- Pest Monitoring: High moisture increases sucking pest activity. Monitor cotton and chilli fields daily.
- Delay Spraying: Avoid foliar chemical sprays immediately before heavy rainfall events.
