AgriTrace Soil Health & Maintenance
===================================
Healthy soil holds moisture, supports crop roots, and contains balanced nutrients.
- pH Regulation: Add lime (calcium carbonate) to raise pH in acidic soils. Add gypsum (calcium sulfate) or sulfur to lower pH in alkaline soils.
- Cover Cropping: Plant legumes (beans/peas) to fix atmospheric nitrogen naturally.
- Mulching: Prevents soil erosion, retains soil moisture, and controls weeds.
