AgriTrace Weather Advisory: Drought Mitigation
==============================================
Drought stress reduces crop yields and dehydrates root systems.
Mitigation Strategies:
- Drip Irrigation: Delivers water directly to root zones, reducing evaporative loss by up to 60%.
- Mulching: Spread organic straw mulches to insulate soil and reduce evaporation.
- Alternate Wetting and Drying (AWD): A water-saving technology in rice fields to reduce water consumption.
