AgriTrace Fungal Disease Manual
===============================
Fungal pathogens thrive under high humidity, wet foliage, and poor ventilation.
Key Diseases:
1. Rice Blast (Magnaporthe oryzae): Diamond-shaped lesions on leaves. Treat with Tricyclazole or spray Pseudomonas fluorescens.
2. Potato Late Blight (Phytophthora infestans): Decaying dark patches on leaves. Apply Metalaxyl or copper fungicides.
3. Anthracnose: Sunken black spots on fruits. Practice clean crop rotations and apply Carbendazim.
