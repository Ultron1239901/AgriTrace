AgriTrace Crop Manual: Cotton (Gossypium)
=========================================
Cotton is a major cash crop requiring high temperatures, moderate rainfall, and abundant sunshine during growth.
Soil: Deep black clayey soils (Regur) or alluvial soils with good drainage capacity are perfect.
Sowing: Sown during May-June. Spacing is kept at 90cm x 60cm.
Pest Alert: Highly susceptible to Bollworm complex. Deploy pheromone traps and use Bt cotton seeds for prevention.
