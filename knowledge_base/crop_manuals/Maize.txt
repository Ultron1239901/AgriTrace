AgriTrace Crop Manual: Maize / Corn (Zea mays)
==============================================
Maize thrives under temperatures of 21-27°C and requires 50-100cm of rainfall.
Soil: Fertile alluvial or red loamy soils with good aeration.
Sowing: Sown in Kharif (June-July) or Rabi (October-November) seasons. Spacing: 60cm x 20cm.
Key pests: Fall Armyworm (FAW) is a major threat. Intercrop with cowpea or deploy biological control agents.
