AgriTrace Crop Storage Guidelines
=================================
Proper storage prevents post-harvest weight loss and quality deterioration.
- Grains (Rice/Wheat): Dry to <14% moisture. Store in clean, airtight silos or pesticide-treated jute bags. Keep temperature at 20-25°C and relative humidity at 60-65%.
- Potatoes: Store in cold storage units at 4-7°C with 90% relative humidity to prevent sprouting and rotting.
- Fruits/Vegetables: Store in cool, ventilated crates to reduce respiration rate.
