AgriTrace Irrigation Standards
==============================
Efficient watering protects crops and saves resources.
1. Sprinkler Irrigation: Best for sandy loams and close-growing crops like wheat or leafy vegetables.
2. Drip/Trickle: Ideal for orchard crops, banana, and wide-spaced rows.
3. Furrow Irrigation: Traditional watering for ridge crops like potato. Ensure furrows are graded properly.
