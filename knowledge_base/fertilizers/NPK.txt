AgriTrace Fertilizer Guide: NPK Balance
=======================================
Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K) are the primary crop nutrients.
- Nitrogen: Promotes vegetative leaf growth. Excess causes soft foliage, making crops susceptible to fungal diseases like rust/blast.
- Phosphorus: Builds strong roots and enhances early plant development.
- Potassium: Improves disease resistance, water regulation, and fruit quality.
Apply based on soil testing reports to avoid groundwater pollution.
