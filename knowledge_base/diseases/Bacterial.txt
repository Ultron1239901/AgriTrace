AgriTrace Bacterial Disease Manual
==================================
Bacterial diseases enter plants through wounds or natural openings (stomata/hydathodes).
Key Diseases:
1. Bacterial Leaf Blight of Rice (Xanthomonas oryzae): Transmitted via splash water. Causes straw-colored leaf wilting.
   - Treatment: Suspend nitrogen applications, drain water, and spray Streptocycline antibiotic (0.1 g/L).
2. Bacterial Canker (Clavibacter): Yellow halos on tomato leaves. Sanitize pruning tools.
