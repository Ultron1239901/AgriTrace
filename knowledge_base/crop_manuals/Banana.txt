AgriTrace Crop Manual: Banana (Musa)
====================================
Banana is a tropical crop requiring warm, wet conditions (20-30°C) and rich, organic, well-draining soils.
Irrigation: High water demand. Drip irrigation is highly recommended to maintain uniform soil moisture.
Management: De-suckering (removing excess side shoots) and propping (supporting heavy fruiting stems).
Key diseases: Panama wilt (Fusarium oxysporum) and Sigatoka leaf spot. Rotate crops and use disease-free suckers.
