AgriTrace Micronutrient Guide
=============================
Crops require trace minerals in minor quantities for metabolic functions.
- Zinc (Zn): Critical for hormone production. Deficiency causes little-leaf disease in cotton/citrus.
- Iron (Fe): Vital for chlorophyll synthesis. Deficiency causes yellowing of young leaves (chlorosis).
- Boron (B): Required for flower pollination and seed setting. Deficiency causes fruit cracking.
