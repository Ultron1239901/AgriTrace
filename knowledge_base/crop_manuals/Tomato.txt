AgriTrace Crop Manual: Tomato (Solanum lycopersicum)
===================================================
Tomatoes require a warm, sunny climate (21-24°C) and well-drained, fertile loamy soils with a pH of 6.0 to 6.8.
Watering: Supply constant moisture (1-1.5 inches of water per week) but avoid waterlogging to prevent root rot.
Key Varieties: Pusa Ruby, Arka Vikas, Roma, and cherry varieties.
Sowing: Seedlings are raised in nurseries and transplanted after 25-30 days. Maintain spacing of 60cm x 45cm.
Nutrient Requirements: Requires rich organic compost, followed by balanced NPK applications at flowering.
