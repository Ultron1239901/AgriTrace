AgriTrace Crop Manual: Rice / Paddy (Oryza sativa)
==================================================
Rice is a primary staple crop thriving in hot, humid climates with average temperatures between 20-37°C.
Soil: Clayey or silty soils that can retain water are ideal. Submergence under 5-10cm of standing water is standard.
Farming Methods: Transplanting, Direct Seeded Rice (DSR), and System of Rice Intensification (SRI).
Harvesting: Harvest when panicles turn golden yellow and moisture content drops below 20%. Dry to 14% for storage.
