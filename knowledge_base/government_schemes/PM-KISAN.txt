Government Scheme: PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)
=============================================================
An initiative by the Government of India providing financial support to small and marginal landholding farmers.
- Benefit: Direct income transfer of INR 6,000 per year in three equal installments of INR 2,000 directly into the bank accounts of landholder farmer families.
- Eligibility: Landowner farmer families with cultivable land holdings registered in land records.
